Sensei Srijan Srivastava

Born and raised in Ram Janki Nagar, Gorakhpur, Sensei Srijan Srivastava is the founder and sole head instructor of Karate Srijan Academy. With over 20 years of teaching experience, he holds a 6th Dan black belt in Shotokan Karate and has trained under Japanese masters.

His teaching philosophy combines traditional discipline with modern self‑defence techniques, ensuring every student builds confidence, fitness, and unshakable spirit. He personally guides each student from white belt to black.
